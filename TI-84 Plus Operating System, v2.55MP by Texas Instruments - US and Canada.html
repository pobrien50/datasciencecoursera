Feature Summary

The latest TI-84 Plus operating system 2.55MP update* features MathPrint™ and is available to download free for TI-84 Plus and TI-84 Plus Silver Edition graphing calculators.  

  • With MathPrint, educators and students can input and view math symbols, formulas and stacked fractions exactly as they appear in textbooks.
  • Select between the default MathPrint mode and optional Classic mode, which displays symbols and fractions on a single line.
  • Easy-to-use 2-D templates allow for the selection of fraction output as improper fractions or mixed numbers.
  • Scroll through the history of calculations on the home screen in MathPrint or Classic mode. Quickly recall an input/output by simply highlighting it and pressing enter.**

* Please update your Catalog Help to version 1.1 for compatibility with TI-84 Plus operating system update, version 2.53MP or higher.

** Lists and matrices as output will not paste from the history.


Steps to check your OS version:

  1. Press 2nd Mem on your TI-84 Plus family calculator
  2. Select 1:About
  3. Press Enter
  4. You should see TI-84 Plus 2.55MP

*Before you begin to install any new Apps or update your OS, be sure you have a new set of batteries in your TI calculator for optimum performance.
